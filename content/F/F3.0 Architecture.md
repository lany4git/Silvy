# Architecture
- **Architecture** is not just the act of building; it is about connecting disciplines, materials, and humanity. It begins with the architect's vision, shaped by the constraints of engineering and code, the personality of materials, and the way light and shadow work throughout a space. These elements come together to create forms that evoke emotion, challenge perceptions, and inspire behavior. The built environment influences how societies grow, shaping culture and leaving marks on history, while being in harmony with and often reshaping nature. It is important to understand the interplay of ideas, emotions, and contexts woven into the fabric of human experience.
