## 4. Comfy at home (Dream home)
![[MB_comfy.png]]
### Outfit Breakdown
- **Closet:** Walk-in. Street boho. Neon sign. Plushy stepping stool to reach high places. Hardwood floor. Boho rug.
- **Staircase:** Stained-glass windows to make the walking experience fun.
- **Kitchen:** (See kitchen)
- **Bathroom:** Tub that's designed like a hot tub that's easy to walk into and out of. Walk-in-shower. sliding door to a balcony for fresh air.
- **Library:** Comfy chair. Books sorted by light to colorful to dark.
- **Laundry room:** Tile floors. Located on the first floor. Open shelves. Storage bins. Beanbags. Soft rug. 
- **Garage:** Spacious. Gym supplies nearby. Workbench nearby. Minimalistic.
- **Dining room:** Don't need comfy chairs. Gold accents. Big enough for friend-gatherings. Curtains. 
- **Balcony:** Has a ledge to put heavy plant pots. Big enough to fit a projector screen and an air-mattress for outdoor movie nights. Towel rug.
- **Bedroom:** Ideally 4-bedrooms. Dark sheets and pillows. Fairy lights. Plants. Bed placed near the window, looking out at trees or nature. 
- **Office:** (See Sci-Fi)
- **Overall style:** Brick, wooden, steel, large windows for natural light
### Vibe Description
Once I design and find a contractor to build this house, I will start allowing people to rent it out. Humble yet experiential. Homey yet theatric. Comfortable yet eccentric.
### Mood
- Disney character: Jemma Simmons (Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D)
- Song: Margaret - Lana Del Rey, Bleachers
