# **Core Values**
### **Integrity**
I live by what I believe in, even when it’s uncomfortable. I want my actions, words, and decisions to line up, whether or not anyone’s watching. Integrity means being honest with myself first, and then with others.
### Family and Relationship
Family, close friends, and the people I trust: they’re my foundation. I care deeply about building meaningful connections that are built on love, respect, and mutual growth.
### Collaboration and Teamwork 
I believe the best things happen when people work together an listen to each other. I value teams where everyone brings something unique and no one is trying to overshadow anyone else. It’s not about ego.
### Achievement and Ambition
I’m driven because I want to use my talents fully and create things that matter to myself and others.
### Novelty
I crave new experiences: new places, new ideas, new versions of myself.
### Creativity and Expression
Whether it’s through music, art, design, writing, or architecture, I feel most joy when I’m expressing something meaningful.
- Go to [[W7.0 Browser Bookmarks|Bookmarks]] for some creative folder titles.
### Curiosity and Open-Mindedness
I’m always asking questions and looking beneath the surface. I don’t claim to have all the answers. I’d rather stay open. Curiosity keeps me learning, evolving, and breaking past assumptions.
### Health (Mental and Physical)
Taking care of my body and mind isn’t optional. It’s what allows me to show up fully. I care about my energy, my movement, my well-being. When I feel strong and well, I can create, connect, and live with intention.
### Growth
I want to grow in how I think, how I act, and how I show up for myself and others.
### Learning
I’m a lifelong learner. I relish learning in all aspects, whether that's through books, people, past mistakes, new skills, or research. Learning keeps me sharp, adaptable, and connected to the world around me.
