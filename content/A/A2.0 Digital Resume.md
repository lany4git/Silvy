#### Bikestop Project ([[A3.04 Restore]]) | September 2024-December 2024
Designed a culturally responsive bike-stop situated on Native American land, integrating tipi-inspired massing with modern form. Met ADA standards and included amenities such as a bike charging station, seating with wheelchair access, air pump, signage, and trail map. Utilized hand-drafting, AutoCAD, and SketchUp to develop and present the design.
#### Bookstore Project ([[A3.03 Helix]]) | April 2024-May 2024
Designed a biomimicry-inspired bookstore featuring organic forms such as a spiral staircase, helical windows, and a curved bookshelf. Developed massing in SketchUp and fully modeled the existing structure in Revit, including complex elements like staircases, custom balconies, beams, and site plans. Navigated spatial constraints, ADA requirements, and structural limitations (e.g., an immovable diagonal beam) to achieve accessible, functional design. Created custom Revit extrusions for unique interior elements and produced final renders using Enscape.
#### Sustainable Cabin Project ([[A3.01 Haven]]) | April 2024-May 2024
Designed a 500 sq. ft. off-grid cabin in Medford, Oregon, intended as a peaceful retreat from city life. Focused on passive design strategies including thermal mass, high R-value wall systems, optimized solar orientation, and partial earth-sheltering, minimizing reliance on fossil fuels. Created a three-zone spatial layout transitioning from privacy to openness: living space, food prep, and family gathering area. Adapted an initially oversized design through creative spatial reconfiguration while preserving its form. Produced technical drawings in Revit and final renders in Unreal Engine 5 and Photoshop.
#### Personal Rhino Project ([[A3.0 Sublime]]) | March 2024-April 2024
Conceptualized a biophilic space dedicated to metal, physical, and spiritual well-being. Designed a dynamic form anchored a circular ritual path, minimal central structure, and long weaving walkways to promote reflection and movement. Refined the layout to address scale and spatial flow, transitioning from an enclosing courtyard to a structure embedded within a rounded path. Modeled complex geometries in Rhino3D using SubD and NURBS tools, and translated the design into Unreal Engine 5 despite significant file size and import challenges. Developed a companion Revit model using in=place masses, custom mullion systems, and expanded axonoometirc drawings to further explore architectural explorer.
