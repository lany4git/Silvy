# **A0.0 — About Me**
_The project is myself._
## **Overview**
Welcome to my creative workspace: a living archive of my thoughts, projects, passions, and processes. I’m a multidisciplinary thinker and maker who explores the intersections of [architecture, art, music, writing, code, and animation](https://silvy.glitch.me/). My work aims to reflect a balance between logic and imagination, structure and spontaneity, clarity and complexity.

This site is organized like a set of construction documents; for instance, each project, idea, or experiment is given a label, a structure, and a place.
## **Core Values**
- **INTEGRITY**  
    I live by what I believe in, even when it’s uncomfortable. I want my actions, words, and decisions to line up, whether or not anyone’s watching. Integrity means being honest with myself first, and then with others.
- **FAMILY AND RELATIONSHIPS**  
    Relationships are everything to me. Family, close friends, and the people I trust—they’re my foundation. I care deeply about building meaningful connections that are built on love, respect, and mutual growth.
- **COLLABORATION AND TEAMWORK**  
    I believe the best things happen when people work together and actually listen to each other. I value teams where everyone brings something unique and no one is trying to overshadow anyone else. It’s about shared energy, not ego.
- **ACHIEVEMENT AND AMBITION**  
    I’m ambitious, but not because I need to prove something. I’m driven because I want to use my talents fully and build things that matter. I set big goals because growth excites me and I want to make an impact.
- **ADVENTURE AND EXPLORATION**  
    I crave new experiences—new places, new ideas, new versions of myself. Life feels most alive when I’m exploring something unfamiliar. Adventure, to me, isn’t just about travel. It’s about discovering something deeper.
- **CREATIVITY AND EXPRESSION**  
    Creating is how I process the world. Whether it’s through music, art, design, writing, or architecture, I feel most alive when I’m expressing something meaningful. It’s my way of turning thoughts and emotions into something others can connect with.
- **CURIOSITY AND OPEN-MINDEDNESS**  
    I’m always asking questions and looking beneath the surface. I don’t claim to have all the answers. I’d rather stay open. Curiosity keeps me learning, evolving, and breaking past assumptions.
- **PEACE AND TRANQUILITY**  
    I value stillness. I’m someone who creates and moves a lot, but I also need moments of quiet to stay grounded. Peace isn’t about avoiding chaos. It’s about coming back to center, even when life gets loud.
- **HEALTH AND VITALITY**  
    Taking care of my body and mind isn’t optional. It’s what allows me to show up fully. I care about my energy, my movement, my well-being. When I feel strong and well, I can create, connect, and live with intention.
- **HAPPINESS AND JOY**  
    Joy matters to me. It’s not just something I stumble into. It’s something I choose to notice and prioritize. I believe in laughter, lightness, and finding moments of happiness even when things are complicated.
- **BALANCE AND WELL-BEING**  
    I don’t believe in burning out just to feel productive. I believe in balance between work and rest, ambition and presence, giving and receiving. It’s about building a life that feels sustainable and fulfilling.
- **GROWTH**  
    I’m here to evolve. I’m not interested in staying the same just because it’s safe. I want to grow in how I think, how I act, and how I show up for myself and others. I believe growth is what makes life meaningful.
- **LEARNING**  
    I’m a lifelong learner. Whether it’s through books, people, mistakes, or new skills, I’m always soaking in something. Learning keeps me sharp, adaptable, and connected to the world around me.
- **SELF-AWARENESS**  
    Knowing myself is the key to everything else. I reflect a lot on my patterns, my strengths, my wounds because the more I understand myself, the more intentional and authentic I can be in everything I do.
## **The Naming System**
Each page in this website is organized with a **code-based system** inspired by architectural sheet indexing:

- **A** for Art Projects (Everything I make is an expression of creativity)
- B for Books that I'm currently reading & reviews
- **C** for Construction elements or fundamentals
- D for Diagrams I created
- G for General rules/laws that are applicable to my projects
- I for Inspirations or moodboards that inform my project
- M for Music I composed or wrote
- R for Research that applies to my projects
- T for Tech, tools, and plugins I'm using
- W for Written work by me
