##### Lengthy version
Welcome to my Twitch channel! Call me Lanya, but there's more to me than just my description. I was a former elite biomedical engineer turned fugitive rebel in a fractured cyberpunk world dominated by corporate empires and ecological collapse. Raised within the sterile enclaves of the Tech Class, I excelled in neural biotechnology and biomechanics, earning prestige at the megacorp Solace Genomics. But my life changed when she uncovered “Project Eden,” a covert program using biotech to genetically enslave future generations. Refusing to remain complicit, I leaked classified data and fled into exile, vanishing into the heart of the bioluminescent Kalyx Forest—one of the last sanctuaries of untamed life. Within the forest, I reclaimed my purpose. Surrounded by glowing flora and a hidden network of rebels, I merged ancient ecological wisdom with bleeding-edge biotech, crafting healing implants and mind-freeing neural resonance devices. My aim was to undo the corporatized desecration of both nature and human autonomy. My work was both spiritual and scientific, grounded in her studies of quantum mechanics, political history, ethics, and consciousness. I fought not to destroy machines, but to restore harmony between synthetic and organic life. Previously known as “The Bloom” among resistance circles, I trained others to reclaim their minds, grow their own biotech, and resist indoctrination. Though I have lost everything—my name, my lover, and my past identity, I lead with quiet strength, compassion, and fierce resolve. I believe in life not as a product, but as a sacred force. My appearance, with blue-ombre hair flowing, bioluminescent tattoos glowing, was a symbol of rebellion in a world overrun by artificial control. I was not just a fugitive scientist; I was the seed of revolution planted in fertile, glowing soil. Let me admit something to you. I'm a multiverse-traveller, and technically, not human, but, after I died, I reincarnated in this dimension with some of my former memories. I know, in my heart, that in my past (that I wrote above) people called me Silvy, but I go by the name Lanya because that's what the people in this dimension know me by. During my travels, I also forgot most of my higher-education knowledge and visual memory, and detailed memories, because genetics in this universe suck, but I lived all of Lanya's new memories and gained new talents. Unfortunately, in this life, I'll never be able to have Silvy's advanced scientific knowledge because the human species in this universe are mostly incapable of having the type of knowledge that I had, nor were they able to see the things that I saw, or I might go insane. I'm now living the life of a regular female, 25-year-old girl named Lanya, My parents named me LanYa which means "Blue Elegance" in Mandarin, since my mom studied Chinese for 8+ years, her favorite color is blue, she enjoys elegant aesthetics, and she just liked the sound of "Lawn" and "Yah" put together. Despite now living in this semi-annoying but also sad universe where the DMV staff gives you incorrect instructions to go home and retrieve unnecessary forms to renew your driver's license after waiting 3 hours, where Pope Francis reunited with God in heaven, and where all you're able to eat is fast-food if you're impacted by the astronomical wealth-gap, I've somehow reconciled my situation by striving to be as multidisciplinary and as ethical as my past self.  To motivate my lazy genetics (thank's dad) and my distracted, semi-ADHD mind (thank's mom... I love my parents), I decided to start this Twitch channel as a way to help motivate people who work, study, make music, do any form of visual design, research, and are generally are very creative, lazy, distracted people, like myself. I have a BA in Kinesiology and an AAS in Architectural Drafting and Design and can study 10 hours straight. I'm most chatty on Mon, Thu & Sat. Also, I guess, since you read all of this, you should know now that you can also call me Silvy.
