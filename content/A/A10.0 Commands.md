#### Study stream
- !task {task.name} (example: !task laundry)✅
- !backlog (shows you your backlog)✅
	- !backlog clear (clears backlog)
	- !now {number} (**example**: !now 4)
	- !remove {number} (**example**: !remove 2)
	- !backlog help
	- !display {number} (prints out name of # task)
	- !now skip (skips current task & gets new one)
	- !now raffle (pulls random task from backlog)
	- !now next (pulls next task from backlog)
	- !later {task.name}; {task.name} (adds multiple tasks)
	- (backlog has capacity of 10 and is cleared after 21 days)
	- !pomo (#/#/#) (**example**: pomo 20/4/3)
- !project {project name} (**example**: !project school)
	- !projects (lists your projects)
	- !project remove (removes active project)
	- !fullreset (removes all projects)
	- (you can have 7 additional projects)
	- !project {project.name}: {task.name}; {task name} (adds tasks)
	- !addto {project.name}: {task.name}; {task.name}
	- !getfrom {project.name} (gets next project's task and puts it into active projects)
	- !getfrom {project.name}: #; # (get's #'d tasks)
